Jean-Pierre Girard est un auteur français connu pour ses prétendues facultés de psychokinèse et divers phénomènes paranormaux dont il serait l'auteur. Il a acquis une certaine notoriété dans les années 1970, notamment avec des démonstrations où il prétend courber des barres métalliques. Pour les sceptiques, ses «pouvoirs» sont simplement des tours d'illusionnisme.

Jean-Pierre Girard is a French author known for his alleged psychokinesis faculties and various paranormal phenomena that would be attributed to him. In the 1970s he gained a certain notoriety, especially for demonstrations in which he claimed to bend metal bars. For sceptics, his "powers" are simply illusionist tricks.
Biography
Childhood
Born on 22 December 1942 in Loches, Indre-et-Loire, as a ward of the Assistance Publique, Jean-Pierre Girard claims that paranormal faculties apparently manifested themselves in him at the age of 7, after he was struck by lightning.
