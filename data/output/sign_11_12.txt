Summer EBT is a one-time $120 benefit for each eligible child to help cover the cost of food during the summer months when school is out.
Our records show that you have not used all of your child's Summer EBT benefit. Any unused benefits will expire on November 28, 2024 and cannot be replaced. Please be sure to use all benefits before they expire.
Your child's benefits were issued to their existing Pandemic-EBT card. Once you receive the replacement card, follow these instructions carefully to set a PIN and activate the EBT card.
