Dear EOPS Student,
Please be advised that the second counseling meeting deadline for EOPS is November 1.
If you haven’t had an opportunity to see EOPS counselors in fall, you can still complete your required meetings in time.
All EOPS students are expected to visit their EOPS counselors three times a semester by the set deadlines and complete at least 6 semester units to maintain a good standing with EOPS and be eligible for EOPS grant and book voucher and other services. For complete details refer to the EOPS Mutual Responsibility Contract (MRC).
You may also click here to watch a helpful video explaining the EOPS MRC.
Your winter priority registration date is now available on your student portal MyGCC.
Mark your calendar for the EOPS priority registration dates for winter and spring:
Winter 2025- October 14
Spring 2025– November 18
This information is also available on the EOPS website under EOPS Calendar.
As you know, we offer EOPS counseling IN-PERSON, ONLINE (zoom), and REMOTELY (by-phone).
Please make sure to complete your online EOPS Appointment Request Form asap, and we will get back to you with your scheduled counseling appointment date and time.

Caro studente EOPS,

Inverno 2025 - ottobre 14 
Primavera 2025 - novembre 18 
Questa informazione è disponibile anche sul sito web EOPS sotto Calendario EOPS.
Come sai, offriamo consulenza EOPS DI PERSONA, ONLINE (zoom), e DA REMOTO (telefonicamente).
Assicurati di compilare il prima possibile il tuo Modulo di Richiesta di Appuntamento EOPS, e ti ricontatteremo con la data e l’orario del tuo appuntamento di consulenza programmato.
