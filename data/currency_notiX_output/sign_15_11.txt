Dear students, you are invited at the Rimini Campus welcoming Party.
From the 21st to the 23rd of October the University of Bologna will host the Alma Mater Fest, a welcome party for all the students.
3 days of: city guided tour, Campus tour, a performance lesson with aperitif and live music, a piadina workshop, an international festival, visits to laboratories, lots of sports activities and information booths.
Don’t miss the performance class “In search of the forgotten classroom: tales of the university life” that will open the Festival on the 21st of October at 05.00 pm. It will be the joining of all your stories. Let's build a show together starting with your tales.
Aperitif with live music reserved to the participants will follow.
Please note that all the activities are by appointment only.

Let's get to know each other and discover the Campus together!
