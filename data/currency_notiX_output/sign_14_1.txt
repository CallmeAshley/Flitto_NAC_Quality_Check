a. Kesepahaman Pengkategorian Jenis Produk pada KMA 748 tahun 2021

a. Unison on Product Type Categorization on KMA 748 of 2021
1. Products made from cereal raw material
a. If it's used as beverage ingredient, then it's categorized as beverage ingredient
group. Example: pearls, bubble tea,
b. If it's used as food ingredient, then it's categorized as cereal and
cereal product. Example: tapioca pearl, pearl sago porridge, salak seed compote.
Response:
a. Pearls, bubble tea, tapioca pearl, pearl sago porridge, and salak seed compote products in
packed condition are included as cereal and cereal product product type.
