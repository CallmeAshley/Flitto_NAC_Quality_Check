Dear students,
We are very pleased to invite you to attend the first of our Industry and Academic Insights lecture sessions, exclusively for Durham University business students. This is an optional series of face-to-face talks from visiting speakers on Wednesdays in terms 1 and 2 to discuss contemporary management and marketing practice and to inspire our business students with ideas for future careers and projects. This session will be held in Waterside Building 1001 (first floor) at 1pm on 30th October and seats will be available on a first-come, first-served basis (no need to book). You will have the opportunity to ask our speakers questions at the end or to email questions to me in advance that I can ask during the session on your behalf.

Chers étudiants,
Nous sommes très heureux de vous inviter à assister à la première session de notre série de conférences Perspectives industrielles et académiques, exclusivement réservée aux étudiants en commerce de l'Université de Durham.
