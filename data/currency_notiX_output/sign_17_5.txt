BMI(체질량지수):
BMI는 신장과 체중을 기준으로 계산되며, 과체중과 저체중의 위험성을 평가하는 기본적인 지표입니다. BMI는 18.5에서 24.9 사이가 정상 범위로 간주됩니다. 하지만 근육량이 많은 운동선수나 체형이 다른 사람에게는 BMI만으로 체중을 판단하는 것이 적절하지 않을 수 있습니다.
체지방률:
체중보다 체지방률이 더 중요한 지표로 여겨지기도 합니다. 체지방률은 근육과 지방의 비율을 나타내며, 일반적으로 남성의 경우 10-20%, 여성의 경우 18-28%가 건강한 범위로 여겨집니다.
신체 조성(BMI와 근육량):
신체 조성은 근육량과 지방량의 비율을 고려한 것입니다. 체중이 동일하더라도 근육이 많은 사람과 지방이 많은 사람의 건강 상태는 다를 수 있으므로, 이상 체중을 설정할 때는 근육량과 지방량도 함께 고려해야 합니다.

BMI (Body Mass Index):
BMI is calculated based on height and weight and is a basic indicator for assessing the risks of being overweight or underweight. BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered within the normal range. However, for athletes with a high muscle mass or individuals with different body types, BMI alone may not be an appropriate measure for evaluating body weight.
Body Fat Percentage:
Body fat percentage is considered a more important indicator than weight. It represents the ratio of muscle to fat, and generally, a healthy range is 10-20% for men and 18-28% for women.
Body Composition (BMI and Muscle Mass):
Body composition refers to the ratio of muscle mass to fat mass. Even if two individuals have the same weight, the health condition of someone with more muscle mass may be different from someone with more fat. Therefore, when setting an ideal body weight, muscle and fat mass should also be considered.
4. Reasons for Setting an Ideal Body Weight:
Setting an ideal body weight is not only about appearance or body shape but is a strategy for optimizing both physical and mental health in the long term. The following reasons are particularly important when determining an ideal body weight.
Health Improvement:
Maintaining an appropriate weight enhances cardiovascular health, metabolic health, and immune function. This goes beyond merely preventing diseases and contributes to improving the quality of life.
Mental Stability:
Reaching and maintaining a desired weight provides a sense of achievement, and through self-management, one can experience psychological stability. This helps strengthen self-esteem and reduces stress in daily life.
