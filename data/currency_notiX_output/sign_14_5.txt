As we approach the period around November 30, 2023, we will be transitioning our Agency System from the previous agency company (PT Perusahaan Pelayaran Nusantara Panurjwan / PPNP) to the new agency company (PT MSC Mediterranean Shipping Indonesia). During this transition, we would like to inform you of the following:
PT. PERUSAHAAN PELAYARAN NUSANTARA PANURJWAN
As Agent for Mediterranean Shipping Company S.A
18, Mampang Prapatan, Jakarta 12710. INDONESIA
TEL: +62-21 50805000 FAX: +6221 50805001 W: www.msc.com Page 2 of 3
Sensitivity: Internal
a. From November 30, 2023, until December 1, 2023, at 09:00 (Tentative), MSC will not issue or send invoices (including additional invoices) to customers due to the system migration. After the aforementioned time (tentative), MSC will issue and send invoices to customers with the new invoice letterhead (Mediterranean Shipping Company SA).
b. The currency exchange rate for imports with an estimated time of arrival ("ETA") on November 30 and December 1 – 4, 2023, will be based on the exchange rate as of November 27, 2023. Starting December 5, 2023, the currency exchange rate will again be based on the rate 4 days before the ETA.
c. Customers can collect the Bill of Lading from the counter on November 30, 2023, as usual, provided that payment has been received in the bank account listed on the invoice.
