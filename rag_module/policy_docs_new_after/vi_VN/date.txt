[Date Format]

Definition:  
Vietnamese dates follow the **Day → Month → Year** order.  
If a weekday is present, it must appear **before** the date and followed by a comma.

Examples:  
- Thứ hai, ngày 28 tháng 7 năm 2025  
- 28 tháng 7 năm 2025  
- 28 thg 7, 2025  
- 28/07/2025

Evaluation Criteria:
- Confirm the Day → Month → Year order.
- If a weekday is present, it appears before the date with a comma.
- Accept both full-form and abbreviated styles.
- Do not generate a weekday if it is not in the source.
- **Do not generate a year unless it exists in the source.**

---


[Exceptions]

- Do not apply the formatting rules to numbers that are part of:
  - telephone numbers
  - addresses
  - identification codes
  - or clearly unordered lists of numbers with no separators

- If the numbers or formats (e.g., 10:00, 12/31, $15) appear to be part of such non-linguistic constructs based on the sentence context, do not apply localization guidelines.

- Always consider the context of the original sentence before making changes.
